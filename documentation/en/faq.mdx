## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where can I find the statistics for all my organizations?
The **Dashboard** section shows your organization's statistics as charts.  
If you belong to more than one organization, click the **"Switch Organization"** button to choose the agency whose statistics you want to see.

### How can I manually evaluate a Security Framework?
Go to the **"Associations"** page under the **"Cyber Assurance"** section in the left-hand menu.  
There, click the **"Evaluate"** button and fill in the form that sets the risk level of your Security Framework.

### How do I create a Security Framework?
Go to the **"Security Frameworks"** section in the left-hand menu under **"Cyber Assurance"**.  
Click the **"New"** button and fill in the required fields.

### How can I check for threats related to URLs, files, and IPs?
Open the **"Threat Intelligence"** section in the left-hand menu under **"Perimeter Protection"**.  
There you can analyze URLs, files, and IPs to spot anomalies and threats.

### How do I check the status of my web app?
Go to the **"Cloud Defender"** section under **"Perimeter Protection"** and click the **arrow icon** to see detailed status information.

### How frequently is Cloud Defender data updated?
Cloud Defender data is refreshed **every 5 minutes**.

### How can I create an Access Token?
Click your **profile** in the **upper right corner**, then click the **"New"** button and name the token.
