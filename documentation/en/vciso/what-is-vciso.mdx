Aegister's **Virtual CISO** strengthens your security posture. It supports your internal team by managing risks, applying best practices, and keeping you current on threats. Use it to protect your data and build your digital resilience.

![Virtual CISO](/images/vciso.png)

## Key Features

- **Strategic and operational consulting:**  
  The vCISO helps you identify risks, build mitigation plans, and apply sound security practices.

- **Continuous threat monitoring:**  
  The service analyzes current threats and delivers timely updates and early interventions.

- **Security policy development and updates:**  
  The vCISO helps you write and update security policies that comply with current regulations and international standards.

## Benefits of the vCISO Service

- **Access to expertise:**  
  Gain specialized cybersecurity skills without the fixed cost of an in-house CISO.

- **Flexibility and scalability:**  
  The service adapts to your organization's needs.

- **Proactive risk management:**  
  The vCISO monitors threats in real time, so you can act fast to prevent incidents and limit the impact of attacks.

With the **Virtual CISO**, Aegister helps companies build a secure, resilient digital environment, so you can focus on your core business instead of the daily work of managing cybersecurity.
