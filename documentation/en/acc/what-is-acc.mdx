The **Aegister Cyber Console (ACC)** is a web platform that combines human expertise with automation to deliver integrated cybersecurity solutions.

![Services](/images/acc-dark.png)

## Key Features

- **Crowd Security:**  
  Members of the Aegister and OneFirewall Alliance share threats in real time, backed by threat intelligence and proprietary IP reputation algorithms.

- **Artificial Intelligence:**  
  Analytical and generative models improve risk assessments, remediation plans, and the automation of defense systems.

- **Expert Support:**  
  A team of qualified professionals helps with risk analysis, incident management, and the development of security policies and regulatory compliance.

## Benefits

- **Minimized Downtime:**  
  Fast attack mitigation keeps operations running and protects customer experience and trust.

- **Regulatory Compliance:**  
  ACC helps you meet data protection regulations with security measures that guard against unauthorized access and breaches.

- **Cost Efficiency:**  
  Integrate multiple cloud providers to optimize resources and cut costs without losing performance.

With the Aegister Cyber Console, businesses monitor their security posture in real time, make informed decisions, and defend against cyber threats.
