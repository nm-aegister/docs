This guide shows how to create an account and access the Aegister Cyber Console (ACC) using either of the two registration methods: with Single Sign-On (SSO) or without it.

## Scenario A: User Without SSO

If you do not use a Single Sign-On system, follow these steps:

1. **Registration:**  
   Open the registration page:  
   [ACC Registration](https://app.aegister.com/keycloak/realms/aegister/login-actions/registration?client_id=webapp&tab_id=l13Y1WJUB6U)

2. **Filling Out the Form:**  
   Fill in the registration form with the required information to create your account.
   ![Registration Form](/images/acc-register-account.png)

3. **Adding Organizational Details:**  
   After you register, add your organization's details.
   ![Organization Form](/images/acc-organization-details.png)

4. **Review by the Aegister Team:**  
   The Aegister team reviews your account. If your organization is new, they add the licenses your company purchased and activate your account.

## Scenario B: User With SSO

If you use a Single Sign-On (SSO) system, follow these steps:

1. **Accessing the Platform:**  
   Visit [https://app.aegister.com/](https://app.aegister.com/).

2. **Selecting Your SSO Provider:**  
   Click the "Login" button and choose your SSO provider.
   ![SSO Login](/images/acc-login.png)

3. **Verifying Your Information:**  
   - If the Aegister team has already linked your email address to your organization, you gain access to all relevant information and services right away.
   - Otherwise, the Aegister team reviews your account and, if it is a new organization, adds the purchased licenses.

Follow these steps to register and access the Aegister Cyber Console quickly and securely.
