[Aegister Threat Blocker](https://aegister.com/it/solutions/atb/) is an all-in-one device from [Aegister](https://aegister.com) that protects business perimeters. It uses threat intelligence and IP reputation algorithms from [OneFirewall](https://onefirewall.com) to apply real-time threat data and block both inbound and outbound threats automatically.

![ATB](/images/atb.png)

## Key Features

### Automation
- **Automatic threat blocking:**  
  The system detects and neutralizes threats in real time, protecting you continuously.

### Integration
- **Broad compatibility:**  
  ATB fits your existing infrastructure and works with **firewalls, SIEM, SOAR, and WAF**.

### Alliance
- **Partnership with OneFirewall:**  
  Through [OneFirewall](https://onefirewall.com/intelligence.html), ATB draws on threat intelligence that strengthens both prevention and management.

With Aegister Threat Blocker, companies get perimeter protection that adapts as threats change.
