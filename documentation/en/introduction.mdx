Aegister SpA builds and sells cybersecurity solutions for small and medium-sized businesses.
The company combines Crowd Security, Automation, and Artificial Intelligence to deliver services to targeted market segments.
As a spin-off of the University of Salerno, Aegister SpA draws on partners who hold strategic and technological roles across the business.
