Aegister SpA develops and markets comprehensive cybersecurity solutions tailored for small and medium-sized businesses.  
The company leverages advanced technologies—including **Crowd Security, Automation, and Artificial Intelligence**—to deliver effective services within targeted market segments.  
As a spin-off of the University of Salerno, Aegister SpA is supported by distinguished partners who occupy key strategic and technological roles throughout the business.
