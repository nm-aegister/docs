**AegisterVPN** is a data protection app for **Android and iOS** that delivers secure, encrypted connections. It opens a private tunnel between your device and the Aegister VPN server, backed by our Threat Intelligence, so your connection stays safe and your privacy intact on any network.

![Aegister VPN](/images/vpn.png)

## Key Features

- **Secure browsing:**  
  Encrypts your internet traffic and protects your data as you browse.

- **Privacy protection:**  
  Masks your IP address and location for anonymous, secure browsing.

- **Off-network security:**  
  Cuts the risk of interception on public Wi-Fi and keeps no activity logs.

## Benefits of AegisterVPN

- **Strong protection:**  
  All data travels through a secure tunnel, lowering the risk of cyberattacks and eavesdropping.

- **Reliable connections:**  
  Holds a stable, secure connection in the office or while you work remotely.

- **Easy to use:**  
  The app gives you strong protection without extra complexity.
